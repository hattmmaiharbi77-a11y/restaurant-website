Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 2018 by Chef Antoine Dubois, L'Artisan emerged from a vision to create a dining experience that transcends the ordinary. Drawing inspiration from classical French techniques and contemporary global influences, we have cultivated a culinary identity that is both timeless and innovative.

Every element of L'Artisan has been thoughtfully curated — from the hand-blown glassware to the artisanal ceramics, from the ambient lighting to the carefully composed playlists. We believe that true luxury lies in the details.

— Chef Antoine Dubois
